How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to guide the snake. Eat food to grow longer and raise your score. Avoid crashing into walls or yourself.

  • Each food = 10 points
  • Snake grows every time it eats
  • Game gets faster as score increases
  • Classic game over on collision
Single-file Snake game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
